Frequently Asked Questions

Question: I tried locating a beer and I got nothing! What gives?
Answer: We are a start-up company and we rely on people to enter locations for us. If nobody checked in a beer at your location, you would get "No beers at this location" warning.
Question: Brew Search crashes! Uninstall! 0 stars!
Answer: We are at the early stage of our software development and bugs do happen. Help us fix this by going to the "contact us" page and tell us what happened and what mobile device so we can get this issue resolved.
Question: Do I need GPS turned on to check-in places?
Answer: No. However, GPS will give you a more pinpoint location. All you need is internet data.
Question: What version of Android or IOS do you support?
Answer: We support Android KitKat and above. For Apple, iPhone 5 and above.
Question: Beer and brewery data isn't up to date
Answer: Click the 3 dots icon    on the beer and brewery detail page to update. It takes 24 hours for the update to change upon approval.
Question: Will you offer a Windows Phone version?
Answer: No. Technology we use doesn't support Windows phone.
Question: Do you sell my personal data to solicitors?
Answer: No. We hate getting spammed just as much as you do.
Question: Where do you get your beer and location information?
Answer: We use 3rd party websites. We use BreweryDB.com for beer / brewery information. For other locations, maps, and location reviews, we use Google.
Question: I'm traveling overseas. Will Brew Search work in other countries?
Answer: Yes it will. As long as you have data connection, our App will work.
Question: How come I see Nail Bars when I search for bars?
Answer: We know this problem exists. The 3rd party compnay we use to pull data from searches for where the business type is a bar or where the business name has the word "bar". We are currently looking for a solution to fix this.
Question: Your App said this brewery is open. In fact, it's been closed for awhile!
Answer: A lot of new breweries open and sadly, a lot of them go out of business. Flag the brewery as closed on the brewery detail page by clicking on    icon. Review process takes 24 hours and the brewery will be flagged as permanently closed.
Question: There's a beer/brewery that's not on your app. How do I add it?
Answer: You can either contact us and provide us with the name of the beer or brewery if possible and we'll take care of the rest. Or, you can click the "Post" button in the app menu tab to submit a new beer/brewery.
Question: How close do I have to be to check-in at a location?
Answer: 1000 yards.
Question: Someone is giving false info on new check-ins. How can I notify you?
Answer: Click on the user's check-in detail, hit the red flag button, and tell us the reason why. We take users that abuse the system seriously and will have them removed.
