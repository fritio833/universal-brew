Easy to Use

You can quickly find a beer, brewery, or bar within seconds. An App that is fast, clear, and informative is our mission. Also, you can save a beer and locations to make the check-in process easier.

Crowdsourced

Ever had a great brew at a new brewery or bar and wanted the world to know? Everytime you use our app and check-in, the Brew Search community will be able to locate their favorite brews thanks to you!

Social Friendly

When you check-in, you have the option to post your experience on your wall. Notify all your friends what they are missing out when they see you having a great time and invite them over.

Beer Game

When you check-in a beer at a location, you get points. You get more points either by taking a picture, sharing on social media, leaving a rating, or leaving a comment. Points are reset every 30 days. Check out the leaderboard on our App and see if you come up on top.

Achievements

Earn achievements by number of check-ins, drinking beer styles, and checkin-in at events. We'll continue to add more badges in the future so you'll never run out badges to unlock.

Beer Festivals

Keep track of beer festivals happening in your area with our App. Login to our App and see all the beers served at the event, breweries attending, and use our interactive map ( Feature applies to only fesivals we got on board). You can plan ahead before the event, research beers that fit your profile, and a check list of beers you enjoyed at the festival so you can enjoy it later on.
